## What I believe

I'm driven by three things:
- My faith is the core of why I do what I do. I'm a Christian and I believe that God is the good creator of all things, and that our duty in this world is to glorify Him and explore the world He made.
- Curiosity keeps me up late and wakes me up early. I already loved software, but with AI unlocking the ability for anyone to build, if you can dream it, you can try building it.
- Passion keeps my quest for high quality alive. Not everything can be perfect, but every day I'm striving to make what I ship a little better, whether that's rewriting a landing page again or tuning BiblePlan's algorithm long after it technically works.
