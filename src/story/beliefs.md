- My faith shapes how I think about work. I believe God created a world worth exploring, and building software is one of the ways I get to explore it.
- I'm endlessly curious. AI has made it possible for one person to build products that used to require entire teams, and I love learning whatever I need to turn an idea into a product.
- I care about craftsmanship. Whether it's product strategy, design, SEO, or development, I enjoy getting a little better every time I build something.
