## What I believe

Job postings keep asking for "high agency" people and almost never say what they mean. I got tired of the fuzziness and wrote down a definition you can actually test yourself against: agency is taking an ambiguous goal and closing the loop on it, without being managed through every step, in a way that earns you more of those goals.
