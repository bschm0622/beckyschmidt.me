Bridging the gap between present-day functionality and the required future state of the Ad Network suite of products, driven by understanding the evolving needs of our users

Lead extensive discovery by managing internal user forums, aggregating beta launch feedback, conducting user interviews, and conducting competitor research

Create thorough PRDs that can be handed off to engineers for technical scoping

Identifying user pain points through extensive user research

Track feature progress end-to-end, unblock teams, and maintain clear status updates through Linear, our product planning platform

Assist in the execution of the roadmap by delineating milestones and requirements, and by collaborating with the engineering and design teams

Develop and deliver comprehensive training to the internal team, ensuring a thorough understanding and effective communication of all new and updated ad network features and functionalities
